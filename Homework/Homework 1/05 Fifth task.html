Actors who starred in the movie Batman 1989

Michael Keaton

Role: Bruce Wayne / Batman:
Michael Keaton played the dual role of billionaire Bruce Wayne and his alter ego, Batman.
His casting was initially controversial, but he was ultimately praised for bringing
depth and seriousness to the character.

Jack Nicholson

Role: Jack Napier / The Joker
Info: Jack Nicholson portrayed the Joker, the film’s main villain. His performance was iconic—he brought a mix of menace and humor that made his Joker unforgettable.
He received critical acclaim and a record-breaking salary deal.

Kim Basinger

Role: Vicki Vale


Info: Kim Basinger played Vicki Vale, a photojournalist who becomes romantically involved with Bruce Wayne and gets caught in the conflict between Batman and the Joker.
